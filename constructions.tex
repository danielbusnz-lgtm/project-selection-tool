\textbf{\Large Project Selection Tool Setup}
\end{center}

\vspace{0.5em}

\textbf{1. Get a Google Sheet}

\textbf{Option A:} Use the sample sheet (make a copy):\\
\url{https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/128-QI735i0o33xSdy4uvlM2Z7DghWXgcZoIQx736D2A/copy}

\textbf{Option B:} Create your own at sheets.google.com with these columns:

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
category & name \\
\midrule
Vanity & 36" White Shaker \\
Vanity & 48" Gray Flat Panel \\
Countertop & White Quartz \\
Countertop & Gray Granite \\
Sink & White Undermount \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

Add as many rows as you need. Each unique category becomes a step.

\vspace{1em}

\textbf{2. Publish the Sheet}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=2em]
\item File $\rightarrow$ Share $\rightarrow$ Publish to web
\item First dropdown: select your sheet
\item Second dropdown: change to \textbf{Comma-separated values (.csv)}
\item Click \textbf{Publish}
\item Copy the URL
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{1em}

\textbf{3. Open the App}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=2em]
\item Go to: \textbf{project-selection-tool.vercel.app}
\item Paste your URL
\item Click Save \& Continue
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{1em}

\textbf{To Update Products}

Just edit the Google Sheet. Refresh the app to see changes.
